About

Jack Williams Creative was founded by Jack, our main videographer and video editor. After starting his freelance career at the beginning of 2019, creating social media content for a YouTube and Instagram cookery channel, he found a brilliant opportunity to progress with White Villa Films, who he both films and edits for, and has become a core member of the team.

During Jack's time at White Villa Films they have gone on to win 'Wedding/Event Videographer of the Year' at the Bristol Prestige 2020 Awards.

Jack has now decided to take on his own clients alongside others at White Villa Films.
