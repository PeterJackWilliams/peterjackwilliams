About

Jack Williams Creative was founded by Jack, our main videographer and video editor. After graduating from Bath Spa University in Film and television Production, Jack started his freelance career at the beginning of 2019, creating social media content for a YouTube and Instagram cookery channel. He then went onto find a brilliant opportunity to progress with White Villa Films, who he both films and edits for, and has become a core member of the team over the past year.

During Jack's time at White Villa Films they have gone on to win 'Wedding/Event Videographer of the Year' at the Bristol Prestige 2020 Awards.

Jack has now decided to take on his own clients alongside others at White Villa Films.
