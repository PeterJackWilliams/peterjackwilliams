ABOUT

My name is Jack Williams, I am a freelance videographer, video editor and photographer.

In the past year I have learnt a lot by getting my first two freelance opportunities: using various cameras during live events in both videography and photography, developing my video editing knowledge and skills with large and small scale projects, as well as graphic design and illustration.
